<div class="page-header-custom"><h1>Support</h1></div>
<p class="page-meta">Need help? We're here for you.</p>

<div class="faq-item">
  <h3>How do I log a stress moment?</h3>
  <p>Tap the big circle on the main screen. That's it!</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
  <h3>How do I change the breathing technique?</h3>
  <p>Tap the breathing method pill below the main button, or go to <strong>Settings → Breathing Technique</strong>.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
  <h3>Where is my data stored?</h3>
  <p>All data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled, it syncs privately to your other devices.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
  <h3>How do I export my data?</h3>
  <p><strong>Settings → All Logs & Export → Export</strong> (top right).</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
  <h3>How do I cancel my subscription?</h3>
  <p><strong>Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Still Pro → Cancel.</strong></p>
</div>
