# Still Support

Need help with Still? We're here for you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I log a stress moment?
Just tap the big circle on the main screen. That's it!

### How do I change the breathing technique?
Tap the breathing method pill below the main button, or go to Settings → Breathing Technique.

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled, it syncs privately to your other devices.

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings → All Logs & Export → Export (top right).

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Still Pro → Cancel.
