<div class="page-header-custom"><h1>Privacy Policy</h1></div>
<p class="page-meta">Last updated: Febuary 7, 2026</p>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>Your Privacy Matters</h2>
  <p>Still is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your personal wellness data should stay personal.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>What Data We Collect</h2>
  <p>Still collects and stores the following data <strong>locally on your device</strong>:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Stress/calm moments you log (timestamps)</li>
    <li>Notes and tags you add to moments</li>
    <li>Your app preferences and settings</li>
    <li>Breathing exercise completion data</li>
  </ul>
  <p>This data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled).</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>What We Don't Collect</h2>
  <p>We do <strong>not</strong> collect, store, or transmit:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Personal identification information</li>
    <li>Location data</li>
    <li>Device identifiers</li>
    <li>Usage analytics or behavior tracking</li>
    <li>Any data to third-party services</li>
  </ul>
  <blockquote>Still has no servers. Your data never leaves your device except to sync with your own iCloud account.</blockquote>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>iCloud Sync</h2>
  <p>If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This sync is:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>End-to-end encrypted</strong> by Apple</li>
    <li><strong>Accessible only to you</strong></li>
    <li>Subject to <a href="https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/">Apple's privacy policy</a></li>
  </ul>
  <p>You can disable iCloud sync in <strong>Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud</strong>.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>Health Data</h2>
  <p>Still offers optional HealthKit integration. If enabled:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Heart rate data is read to detect stress spikes</li>
    <li>Breathing sessions may be written as Mindful Minutes</li>
    <li>This data stays in Apple's Health ecosystem</li>
    <li>You control all permissions</li>
  </ul>
  <p>HealthKit data is <strong>never</strong> transmitted to us or any third party.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>Biometric Authentication</h2>
  <p>Still offers optional Face ID / Touch ID protection. Your biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave — never accessed or stored by Still. It's used only to unlock the app locally.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>Deleting Your Data</h2>
  <p>You have full control:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Delete individual moments from <strong>History</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Export</strong> all data as CSV anytime</li>
    <li>Uninstall the app to remove all local data</li>
    <li>Disable iCloud sync to stop cloud storage</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We don't retain any data after you delete it.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
  <h2>Policy Changes</h2>
  <p>We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates. Continued use of Still after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.</p>
</div>
