Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Still is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your personal wellness data should stay personal.

What Data We Collect

Still collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Stress/calm moments you log (timestamps)
  • Notes and tags you add to moments
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Breathing exercise completion data

This data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled).

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Any data to third-party services
Still has no servers. Your data never leaves your device except to sync with your own iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This sync is:

  • End-to-end encrypted by Apple
  • Accessible only to you
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy

You can disable iCloud sync in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

Health Data

Still offers optional HealthKit integration. If enabled:

  • Heart rate data is read to detect stress spikes
  • Breathing sessions may be written as Mindful Minutes
  • This data stays in Apple's Health ecosystem
  • You control all permissions

HealthKit data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Biometric Authentication

Still offers optional Face ID / Touch ID protection. Your biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave — never accessed or stored by Still. It's used only to unlock the app locally.

Deleting Your Data

You have full control:

  • Delete individual moments from History
  • Export all data as CSV anytime
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync to stop cloud storage

We don't retain any data after you delete it.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates. Continued use of Still after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
